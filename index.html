About me

I am a filmmaker, photographer, and video editor with over three years of experience in audiovisual production, including one year in motion graphics. I have worked on films, events, corporate videos, and 2D animations, collaborating with major festivals such as the Carthage Film Festival (JCC), Carthage Music Days (JMC), the Carthage Festival, and the Hammamet Festival. With strong expertise in both production and post-production, I am capable of managing large-scale projects and taking full responsibility for their execution, ensuring high quality and creativity at every stage.

Services

Corporate Event Videography

Whether it's a corporate event, conference, or live performance, I provide comprehensive video coverage, capturing key moments and editing them into a cohesive narrative that reflects the energy and atmosphere of the event. Additionally, I offer services in restaurant photography and promotional videos for businesses, whether long or short form, with a focus on meeting client needs and enhancing their message in a creative and professional way.

Video Editing

I provide professional video editing focused on clear and cohesive visual storytelling. From refining raw footage to structuring a well-rounded narrative, I ensure each project reflects its creative vision and objectives. Whether it’s a short film, documentary, promotional video, Reels, or Digital AD, every edit is tailored to enhance the message and make an impact on the target audience.

Audio production and Sound design

I offer complete sound editing and mixing, including cleaning up audio, adding sound effects, and balancing audio levels. With over 10 years of experience, I also specialize in sound design, from creating custom sounds to integrating them seamlessly into your project. This process includes voiceover integration, background music, and ensuring all audio elements are perfectly aligned with the visual aspects of the video, giving your project a polished, professional sound.

Photography

I capture high-quality images that bring your vision to life, whether it's events, portraits, products, real estate, corporate branding, or promotional content. With a keen eye for detail, I focus on lighting, composition, and post-processing, ensuring every shot is visually compelling and professionally polished. My goal is to create images that not only meet your needs but also leave a lasting impact.

Motion Graphics & Animation

I seamlessly integrate motion graphics and animations into your video, from lower thirds and titles to full animated sequences. This enhances the visual experience, making the content more dynamic and engaging, whether for educational, corporate, or creative purposes.

Color grading

I bring footage to life with professional color grading, refining the visual tone and atmosphere of your video. By adjusting exposure, contrast, and saturation, I craft the perfect aesthetic to match your project’s vision. Whether you’re aiming for a bold, cinematic look or a natural, understated style, I ensure the colors enhance the storytelling and overall impact.

Video Optimization

I optimize videos for different platforms, ensuring the best resolution, aspect ratio, and format for high-quality playback. Whether for social media, websites, or broadcast, I tailor each video to meet platform-specific requirements, guaranteeing smooth performance across all devices and networks.

Highlight reel and promos

I specialize in crafting dynamic highlight reels that capture the most impactful moments of your event, product, or performance. Whether you need a high-energy promo or a sleek, polished showreel, I create engaging visuals that captivate your audience and leave a lasting impression.
